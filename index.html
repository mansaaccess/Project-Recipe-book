Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredient Quantity
Spaghetti 200g
Bacon 100g
Eggs 2
Parmesan Cheese 50g
  • Cooking time: 20 minutes
  • Servings: 2

To prepare, cook the spaghetti until al dente. Fry the bacon until crispy, then mix with beaten eggs and Parmesan cheese. Combine with spaghetti and serve warm.

Chicken Stir Fry

Ingredient Quantity
Chicken Breast 250g
Mixed Vegetables 200g
Soy Sauce 3 tbsp
Ginger & Garlic 1 tsp each
  • Cooking time: 25 minutes
  • Servings: 2-3

Stir fry chicken with garlic and ginger until cooked. Add vegetables and soy sauce, cooking until tender. Serve over rice or noodles.

Chocolate Brownies

Ingredient Quantity
Dark Chocolate 200g
Butter 150g
Sugar 200g
Flour 100g
  • Cooking time: 30 minutes
  • Servings: 8

Melt chocolate and butter together. Mix in sugar and flour, then bake at 180°C for 25-30 minutes. Let cool before cutting into squares.
